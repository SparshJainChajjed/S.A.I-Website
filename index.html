FAQ

What hardware is required?

One gateway per site, moisture sensors per zone, and a relay module for each valve. Everything runs off solar or 12–24 V.

Will this work without internet?

Yes. SAI continues with the latest policy and logs locally until connectivity returns.

How do I tune it for my crop?

Pick a crop profile and adjust moitarget bands in the UI. The model learns seasonal behavior over time.

What savings should I expect?

Typical deployments save 20–40% water while keeping yield stable or improved.
